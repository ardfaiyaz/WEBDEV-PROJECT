STUDENT CLEARANCE SYSTEM

Welcome to the Student Clearance Management System. This platform allows students, staff, and departments to manage and monitor clearance progress in real-time.

| ABOUT SYSTEM

The Student Clearance System is managed by the Office of the University Registrar.

Once a student has completed all necessary requirements, they will receive a claim stub, indicating that their clearance is complete and they are done with the process.

If a student encounters an issue, they should proceed to the Registrar.
